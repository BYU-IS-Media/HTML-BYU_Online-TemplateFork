  • Scheduling Exams:

    We are in the process of creating a scheduling tool for professors to schedule their exam dates online. Until that time the BYU Online Testing Center will work with your instructional designer to get your preferred exam dates for each new semester. If there is a conflict in exam deadlines you will be contacted directly to work out an acceptable time.

  • Deadline Extensions:

    To request a change in the exam deadline for a specific student or for an entire section please contact Michelle Smith with the student/section information, exam information, and new deadlines to Michelle Smith at: michelle.l.smith@byu.edu.

  • BYU Online Testing Center Policies:

    If your exam is "open-book" or "open-note" the Morris Testing Center will only allow students to use paper versions of notes unless you indicate otherwise. If you have questions or would like to allow students electronic access to notes please contact Michelle Smith at: michelle.l.smith@byu.edu.

  • Additional Questions:

    All questions or concerns regarding BYU Online exam proctoring should be directed to Michelle Smith at: michelle.l.smith@byu.edu.
